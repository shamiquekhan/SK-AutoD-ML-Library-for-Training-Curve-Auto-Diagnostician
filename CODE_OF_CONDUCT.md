# Code of Conduct

SK-AutoD is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for contributors and users.

## Expectations

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent.
- Help keep discussions inclusive and productive.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that is abusive, discriminatory, or consistently disruptive.
